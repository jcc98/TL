About Me

I've been translating for over 6 months and counting. I started learning Japanese over 4 years ago and passed the JLPT N1 a year ago. I love reading VNs. My favorite ones are Muramasa, Musicus! and Subahibi. I'm currently striving for the Kanji Kentei Jun 2. I've recently started learning type-setting, and I've been having a lot of fun. I've translated quite a few doujins, as well as a short SFW manga. Please check out my translated works below if you're interested!
